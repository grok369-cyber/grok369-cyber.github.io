Our Heritage

Character, then competence — the same conviction Savio opened its doors with in 2016, now taught alongside the tools of a digital century.

History & Values

Founded on Tula Road in Kawempe Division, Savio College began as a single classroom of thirty students and a conviction that discipline and curiosity are not opposites. Nearly two decades on, that founding belief still shapes every lesson plan: we teach children to reason carefully, act honestly, and build responsible characters that merit trust.

Learning Goals

Our first push is not a trend — it's an extension of the same values. Where past generations learned Latin declensions by heart, ours learn to structure an argument in code as rigorously as in prose. Every learner leaves fluent in both.
